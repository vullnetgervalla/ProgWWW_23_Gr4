WHAT WE DO

Some services we provide for our children

At Tiny Stars Foundation, our commitment extends beyond boundaries.
Here are some of the services we provide for our children.

  • Family Support

    We offer vital family support, ensuring a network of care and understanding that strengthens both children and their families.

  • Health Benefits

    Prioritizing well-being, our health benefits program guarantees access to comprehensive healthcare, fostering the physical and mental health of our children.

  • Scholarships

    Education is empowerment. Through scholarships, we open doors to educational opportunities, empowering children to reach their full potential.

  • Therapy

    In personalized therapy sessions, we tailor interventions to each child's unique needs, nurturing growth and enhancing their quality of life.
